About Garbage Classification

Understanding waste management through AI technology

Why Garbage Classification Matters

Our world is changing, and we can help. Sorting garbage is a simple way to make things better. When we recycle, we save our planet's resources. Every piece of trash we sort correctly helps reduce pollution. Small actions by everyone can create a big positive change for our environment.

How AI Helps

Proper waste classification is crucial for effective recycling and environmental protection. AI can help automate this process and educate people about correct waste sorting. Our machine learning model uses EfficientNetV2B2 architecture to accurately classify waste materials into six main categories.

Environmental Impact

By correctly sorting waste, we can significantly reduce landfill burden, conserve natural resources, and minimize environmental pollution. Each correctly classified item contributes to a more sustainable future for our planet.
